# Participant Guide

Thank you for participating in this study! This phone has a research app installed that passively records sensor data in the background. Please use the phone as you normally would for the duration of the study.

## What the app records

- Motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope)
- Which app is in the foreground and for how long
- Screen on/off and unlock events
- Scroll and tap gestures (direction and timing only)

## What the app does NOT record

- Your screen content, text, or messages
- Anything you type (including passwords)
- Your location
- Photos, audio, or video
- No data is sent over the internet -- everything stays on this device

## Important notes

- **Do not uninstall** the "Doomscroll Sensing" app.
- **Do not disable** the Accessibility Service or Usage Access permission in Settings. If you accidentally do, please re-enable them.
- A small persistent notification ("Doomscroll Sensing is running") is normal -- it keeps the sensors active.
- The app uses minimal battery. If you notice unusual drain, please contact the researcher.

## Privacy

- All recorded data is encrypted on the device.
- Your participant ID is a random code -- it is not linked to your name or identity.
- Data is only retrieved by the researcher via USB cable at the end of the study.
- You may withdraw from the study at any time. If you do, all data from your device will be deleted.
